## Part 1: SEO Strategy & Keyword Matrix

### Keyword Strategy Overview

**Search intent buckets:**
1. **Delay/Alert Keywords** — People with immediate need (high intent)
   - "flight delay alerts" / "free flight tracking" / "predict flight delays"
   - Search vol: 2–5K/mo, Competition: Medium, Intent: Very High

2. **Airline-Specific** — Brand-loyal customers (high commercial value)
   - "United flight delay alerts" / "Delta delay prediction" / "American Airlines tracking"
   - Search vol: 500–2K/mo, Competition: Medium, Intent: High

3. **Business Travel** — Target demographic
   - "business travel apps" / "frequent flyer tips" / "how to prevent missed connections"
   - Search vol: 5–10K/mo, Competition: High, Intent: Medium

4. **Problem Keywords** — Awareness stage
   - "why are flights delayed?" / "cost of missed flight" / "flight delay compensation"
   - Search vol: 5–20K/mo, Competition: High, Intent: Low–Medium

5. **Comparison Keywords** — Consideration stage
   - "Flighty vs TripIt" / "best flight tracking apps" / "flight delay prediction tools"
   - Search vol: 500–2K/mo, Competition: Medium, Intent: High

6. **Long-tail Guides** — Educational, builds authority
   - "how to avoid flight delays" / "guide to standby flights" / "what causes weather delays"
   - Search vol: 100–500/mo, Competition: Low–Medium, Intent: Medium

### Growth Hack #4: Viral Referral Loop

**Mechanism:**
- Every user gets a unique referral link
- Friend signs up → Both get $10 credit (or 1 free month)
- Referrer can earn unlimited (no cap)
- Leaderboard (top referrers featured in newsletter)

**Viral coefficient formula:**
- Share rate: 20% (1 in 5 users share)
- Conversion rate: 15% (1 in 7 friends signs up)
- Viral coefficient: 0.20 × 0.15 = 0.03 (3% viral growth)
- With 1K initial users: 30 signups from referrals (Week 1), 31 (Week 2), etc.
- Over 12 weeks: ~600–800 referral signups

**Implementation:**
- In-app referral widget (Share to Twitter, email, link copy)
- Email signature template (auto-includes referral link)
- Leaderboard page (public rankings)
- Monthly rewards (free month for top 5)

**Expected impact:** +600–800 signups via referrals

## Part 5: Conversion Funnel Strategy

### Funnel Metrics (Month 3 Targets)

```
Website Visitors: 3–5K/month
↓
Newsletter Signups: 800–1.2K (15–25% conversion)
↓
Trial Signups: 600–800 (75% email → trial)
↓
Paid Conversions: 200–300 (25–40% trial → paid)
```

### Email Nurture Sequence (Waitlist → Trial)

**Email 1 (Immediate):**
- Welcome + product overview
- CTA: "Start free trial"
- Goal: 30–40% click rate

**Email 2 (Day 3):**
- Social proof (testimonials, accuracy stats)
- Feature highlight: "Know about delays 2 hours early"
- CTA: "See it in action" (video demo)
- Goal: 25–35% click rate

**Email 3 (Day 7):**
- Case study: "How this United frequent flyer saved $1,200/month"
- Urgency: "Early access = 50% off"
- CTA: "Claim your discount"
- Goal: 20–30% click rate

**Email 4 (Day 14):**
- Feature deep-dive: "How accurate is FlyRely?" (64.8%)
- Comparison: "We vs. Flighty / Airline Apps"
- CTA: "Start free trial"
- Goal: 15–25% click rate

**Email 5 (Day 21):**
- Countdown: "48 hours left for early access pricing"
- FOMO: "Join 5,000+ frequent flyers"
- CTA: "Get access now"
- Goal: 25–40% click rate (highest urgency)

### Trial-to-Paid Strategy

**In-app messaging (Days 1–7):**
- "7 days left in your trial" (Day 3)
- "Upgrade before trial ends" (Day 5)
- "Only 1 day left" (Day 6)
- "Your trial expires tomorrow" (Day 7)

**Email sequence (Trial ending):**
- Email (Day 6): "Your trial is ending. Here's what you'll miss"
  - Summary of predictions they've received
  - Value saved (e.g., "You saved 4 hours this week")
  - Upgrade incentive: "50% off annual plan"

- Email (Day 8, after trial): "Last chance: 50% off ends today"
  - Social proof (5K+ users)
  - Risk reversal: "30-day money-back guarantee"
  - Simple CTA: "Upgrade now"

**Conversion targets:**
- 25–40% trial-to-paid conversion
- 50–60% annual plan (vs. monthly)
- 3–5% free trial → paid in month 3
