## Section 7: FAQ

### Q1: How Accurate Is FlyRely?
```
Answer: 64.8% — roughly 2x better than human guessing or industry averages.
Why not higher? Delays are chaotic. Even airlines can't predict perfectly.
Our job is to beat the odds, and we do.

We publish our accuracy publicly. No hidden metrics.
```

### Q2: How Far in Advance Do You Predict?
```
Answer: 1–2 hours before your scheduled departure.
This gives you actionable time: rebook, reschedule, rest.

Longer predictions (3+ hours) exist but are less reliable, so we skip them.
We'd rather be accurate when it matters than noisy all day.
```

### Q3: How Do You Get Your Data?
```
Answer: Real-time flight tracking (OpenSky Network), weather APIs (Tomorrow.io),
historical delay records (BTS), airline schedules, and aircraft performance data.

All third-party sources. Nothing proprietary or insider info.
This means our predictions are unbiased — airlines don't influence our alerts.
```

### Q4: Do You Integrate With My Calendar?
```
Answer: Yes. You can sync Google Calendar, Outlook, or iCal.
We'll reschedule around your meetings, not flight times.
You stay in control — we just make it easier.
```

### Q5: What Happens If a Delay Is Predicted but Doesn't Happen?
```
Answer: We'll show it in your history with a note "Predicted: Yes, Actual: No."
Over time, you'll see exactly how often we're right (and occasionally wrong).
This transparency is why we show accuracy rates.
```

### Q6: Can I Export My Prediction History?
```
Answer: Yes. CSV, JSON, or Apple Calendar export.
Your data is yours. We don't lock you in.
```

### Q7: Do You Charge Per Prediction or Per Flight?
```
Answer: Neither. Monthly or annual subscription.
Unlimited predictions, all your flights. No per-use charges.
```

### Q8: What Airlines Are Supported?
```
Answer: All major US carriers: United, American, Delta, Southwest, JetBlue, Alaska, Spirit, Frontier.
Plus international carriers: Air Canada, British Airways, Lufthansa, Air France, Japan Airlines.
More launching each month.
```

### Q9: Is My Flight Data Private?
```
Answer: Completely. We encrypt all flight data at rest and in transit.
We don't sell your data. We don't share with airlines.
Privacy policy is here [link]. We're SOC 2 certified.
```

### Q10: What If My Prediction API Fails?
```
Answer: You'll get an error message, not a silent failure.
We log every request and retry failed predictions automatically.
Uptime SLA: 99.5% (4 hours per month acceptable downtime).
Status page: [link]
```

### Q11: Can I Share Predictions With Travel Companions?
```
Answer: Yes. Generate a shareable link for any prediction.
They can see the prediction and reasoning without creating an account.
```

### Q12: Do You Offer Group Pricing?
```
Answer: Yes. For teams 5+, we offer 30% discount.
Email hello@flyrely.ai for enterprise terms.
```

## Conversion Strategy

### Goal 1: Waitlist Growth
- Target: 100K waitlist members by launch
- Tactics: Product Hunt, Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit, relevant communities
- Metric: Cost per waitlist signup ($0 organic, track CAC if paid)

### Goal 2: Early Engagement
- Target: 50% of waitlist signs up in first week
- Tactics: Email sequence (5 emails over 3 weeks), SMS alerts
- Metric: Launch-week signups vs. total waitlist

### Goal 3: Retention
- Target: 60% of trial users convert to paid
- Tactics: Email engagement, in-app education, success metrics
- Metric: Paid conversion rate, month 1 churn

### Goal 4: Viral Loop
- Target: 25% of new users come from referrals
- Tactics: Referral bonus (50% off for referrer + referee), shareable predictions
- Metric: Referral-driven signups as % of total

## Design Notes

- **Layout:** Clean, whitespace-heavy (Stripe-like aesthetic)
- **Typography:** Bold headlines, readable body copy (16px minimum)
- **Images:** Real prediction screenshots, not mockups
- **Mobile:** Fully responsive (tested on iPhone 12, 14, Android)
- **Load time:** <2 seconds (optimized images, minimal JS)
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AA compliant (color contrast, alt text, keyboard nav)
- **Analytics:** Segment.com (track page views, form submissions, conversions)
